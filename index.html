Project 2: HyperNarrative

Through this project, I hoped to unpack the beauty of self care. Delving into my hobby of journaling, I wanted to share that revisiting your thoughts and reflecting on the chaotic mess that often overwhelms ourselves allows us to be in control of our emotions. Having a record of my personal thoughts, feelings, and insights written down in front of me allows me to travel back mto my favorite and most fearful memories. I am automatically immersed in the environment I wish to be in with the words that are scribbled onto the blank page. Journaling allows me to delve into an alternate dimension where I am not afraid to speak, think, or act upon anything.

Final: Embrace Yourself

Project 3: Networked Justice

Feeling lost can be a good thing. It means realizing that we’re no longer who we once were, what we once wanted, and who we once wanted to become. It means that we’re starting to change the way we think— and that’s a sign of growth. This website is dedicated as a reminder to myself, as well as many others who feel overwhelmed to belong, that it is okay to not have a direction. Permitting yourself to accept who you’ve grown to be and also question what you want today is a part of the process of ending up somewhere better.
